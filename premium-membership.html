Frequently Asked Questions

How does the free trial work?

Your 7-day free trial begins as soon as you sign up. You won't be charged until the trial period ends, and you can cancel anytime before then. After the trial, your selected plan will automatically renew each month until canceled.

Can I switch between plans?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. If you upgrade, the new plan will take effect immediately. If you downgrade, the change will apply at the start of your next billing cycle.

What are the parental controls?

Our parental controls allow you to set content filters based on age, create viewing time limits, monitor watch history, and block specific content. Premium and Family plans offer more advanced controls, including PIN protection for settings and custom content filters for each child profile.

How do I cancel my subscription?

You can cancel your subscription at any time by going to your Account settings and selecting 'Manage Subscription'. Your premium access will continue until the end of your current billing period.
